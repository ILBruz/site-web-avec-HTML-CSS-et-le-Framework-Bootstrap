What are indicators in trading ?

Indicators in trading are used to identify the direction of the trend. Below you will find indicators with explanations

RSI indicators

Momentum indicators: These indicators evaluate the speed of an asset's price movement and can help identify potential reversal points. Examples include the Stochastic Oscillator and the Relative Strength Index (RSI)

ADX indicator

Trend indicators: These indicators help identify the general direction of an asset's price. Examples include the moving average and the average directional index (ADX)

MFI indicator

Volume Indicators: These indicators analyze the trading volume of an asset to gauge the strength of a price movement. Examples include the Money Flow Index (MFI)
