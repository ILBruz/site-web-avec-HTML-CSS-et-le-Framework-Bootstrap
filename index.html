What is trading?

Trading is an activity of buying and selling financial instruments (such as stocks, options, currencies, cryptocurrencies, futures), the value of which fluctuates during the opening hours of stock exchanges. The trading activity, which thanks to the IT revolution, the abandonment of the physical market and the development of electronic markets has spread into its current form of online trading. This telematic application has expanded trading operations to the study of stock graphs updated in real time and the diffusion of technical analysis, as well as the use of financial simulations whose primary purpose is to support strategic investment planning.
